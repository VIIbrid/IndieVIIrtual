Welcome to IndieVIIrtual!

      This website is the home of VIIbrid and company's content. It will store the posts, images, audio, videos, programs, and 3D models made by the aforementioned people. You can find these items by clicking the respective icons above!

      This place exists in place of the current issue regarding the fact that I had to separate all of my content in order to post it. Images went to DeviantArt, videos to YouTube, music to SoundCloud, games to GameJolt, communication to Discord, and to even network these, I had to use Reddit. This annoyance, on top of the ever-growing frustration due to a multitude of problems regarding the sites themselves, drove me to resolve all of these issues with one stone.
