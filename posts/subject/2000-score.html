      The score for the retro project will be released after the game releases to ensure that the tracks included have first been listened to in their intended place. If it wasn't for the fact that this will be in my official discography, Composition Notes would have released well after the game has.

      It is tricky to pin down exactly what the score will sound like, and it's even trickier, considering the current level of development. Some notable examples that I've been noting include Chopping Mall, A Grand Day Out, Ratchet & Clank, and Justice. It's a difficult balance between 80s sci-fi, 90s EDM, and 2000s industrial; lean to far on any one side, and the tone is compromised.

Status:
In-Dev
      The first step for me is to compose the themes for the different character groups so I can then score for more specific moments in the game. I've not yet developed anything that I am satisfied with, but I do have a lot of failed attempts.

      After some themes are made, they will have unique scoring for different situations. It's also likely that the melodies will be fleshed out and possibly given individual alterations.
